Getting Started

1

Open FameRoom

Launch the app — no account or sign-in needed. Your practice sessions stay private on your device.

2

Set Up Your Stream

Customize your stream name, choose a duration, and get ready. The app uses your front camera to simulate the live experience.

3

Go Live (for Practice)

Tap Go Live and watch as simulated viewers join, comments scroll in, and hearts and gifts appear — just like a real stream.

4

Review & Repeat

End your stream and review your session. Practice as many times as you like until you feel ready for a real audience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does FameRoom actually broadcast to TikTok or Instagram?

No — FameRoom is a practice environment only. Nothing is ever recorded, uploaded, or shared. All viewers, comments, hearts, and gifts are completely simulated on your device.

Why does the app need camera access?

The camera is used to show your face in the practice stream preview — exactly like it would appear in a real live stream. No video is ever recorded or transmitted.

What is Fame Room Pro?

Fame Room Pro is an optional subscription that unlocks unlimited practice streaming sessions. It’s available as a weekly ($6.99/week) or yearly ($29.99/year) plan.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → FameRoom. Alternatively, open the Settings tab inside the app and tap "Restore Purchases" or "Manage Subscription".

I was charged but can’t access Pro features — what do I do?

Go to Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. This will re-apply your subscription. If the issue persists, please contact support.

Is my data private?

Yes. FameRoom doesn’t collect personal data, require an account, or send any information to external servers. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

The app is crashing or not working correctly

Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue continues, please email us with your device model and iOS version and we’ll help right away.
